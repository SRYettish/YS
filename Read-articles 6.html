Kepler-1992 b

Kepler-1992 b is a terrestrial exoplanet that orbits a G-type star. Its mass is 0.693 Earths, it takes 15.6 days to complete one orbit of its star, and is 0.1169 AU from its star. Its discovery was announced in 2023.
